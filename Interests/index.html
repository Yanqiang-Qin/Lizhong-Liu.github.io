I have a wide rage of hobbies, though I am not necessarily good at any of them.

  • Photography
    I have a Sony camera and several lenses, and I enjoy taking photos when doing outdoor activities. You can immediately find some of my works on this website: every banner image is taken and edited by myself. I am better at shooting scenes than portraits, but will be happy to take photos for you if you are willing to be my model.

  • Oil painting
    I learned oil painting for a rather short period, but really love it. I have finished several (very simple) works and they are now on exhibition in my apartment.

  • Rock climbing
    Again, I love rock climbing but am not really good at it. I hold certificates of belaying from several clubs - they simply mean that I am allowed to belay independently.

  • Story-telling
    I guess I do have a talent of making up and telling stories, most of which are fairy tales or have animated figures. However, this “talent” can easily turn out to be wasted since I seldom note down the stories I make. But I can always come up with new stories.

  • Singing
    Not a lot of my friends admire my singing skills, but I do love singing. One of the benefits of living in China is that you can easily find a karaoke and sing for as long as you want. The best part of it: you don’t even have to worry about being mocked, since people do not expect a concert there.
